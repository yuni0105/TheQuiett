New Album

For the thirteen tracks on this LP, The Quiett mainly worked with Prima Vista but also involved up-and-coming young artists. This way, the album is a mix of The Quiett’s style and the current trends, a bit experimental here and there. It has 13 tracks featuring ZENE THE ZILLA, CHANGMO, Byun Un, and more.
